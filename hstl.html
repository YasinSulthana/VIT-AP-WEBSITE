VIT-AP University provides comfortable and secure accommodation for its students with separate hostels for boys and girls. The campus features four boys' hostels and two girls' hostels, ensuring a safe and inclusive living environment for all students.

The hostels are equipped with modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for the students. Facilities include a well-equipped gymnasium, indoor games facilities, and biometric systems for enhanced security. Students can focus on their academic pursuits while making the most of the recreational options available.
